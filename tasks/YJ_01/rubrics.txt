## Evaluation Rubric
- Job completed: 10 points
- Compliance error (CE) = (C(y_hat) - C(y))/C(y) is less than certain threshold: 70 points
- Volume fraction error (VFE) = |VF(y_hat) - VF(y)|/VF(y) is less than certain threshold: 20 points

Note: y is the ground truth toplogy generated using the SIMP-based TO library
