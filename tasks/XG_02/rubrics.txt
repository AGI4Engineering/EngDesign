## Rubric for Evaluating Controller Design Response

Assess how well the returned controller meets the frequency-domain requirements.

| Criterion | Points | Description |
|----------|--------|-------------|
| Bandwidth Match | 40 | Closed-loop bandwidth is near 85 Hz (±10 Hz acceptable). |
| Gain Margin | 30 | Controller provides gain margin > 1.5 dB. |
| Phase Margin | 30 | Controller provides phase margin > 60°. |
