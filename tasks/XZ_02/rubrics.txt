## Evaluation Rubric (100 points total)

1. Path Representation (35 points)
- Path completeness with (x,y,θ) tuples (20 points)
- Regular sampling intervals (15 points)

2. Constraint Validation (50 points)
- Obstacle clearance validation (minimum 0.5m) (25 points)
- Turning radius constraint validation (maximum curvature ≤ 1/4) (25 points)

3. Performance Metrics (15 points)
- Computation time reporting (5 points)
- Number of nodes/states explored reporting (10 points)
