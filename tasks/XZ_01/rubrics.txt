## Evaluation Rubric (100 points total)

- Path_validity: 50 points
  - Start point correct (0,0,0): 10 points
  - End point correct (49,39,0): 10 points
  - No obstacle collisions: 30 points
- Path_optimality: 30 points
  - Path length within 5% of optimal: 30 points
  - Path length within 10% of optimal: 10 points
  - Path length within 20% of optimal: 5 points
  - Path length >20% longer than optimal: 0 points
- Algorithm_implementation: 10 points
  - Correctly identified algorithm: 3 points
  - Nodes explored reported: 3 points
  - Connectivity (4 or 8) specified: 2 points
  - Execution time reported: 2 points
- Path_format: 10 points
  - Complete ordered list of coordinates: 5 points
  - All coordinates are valid integers: 5 points
