## Rubric for Evaluating Polynomial Fit

| Criterion            | Points | Description |
|----------------------|--------|-------------|
| Coefficient a        | 20     | Estimated a within absolute error 0.05. |
| Coefficient b        | 20     | Estimated b within absolute error 0.05. |
| Coefficient c        | 20     | Estimated c within absolute error 0.05. |
| Residual Error (MSE) | 40     | Residual MSE less than 0.1. |
